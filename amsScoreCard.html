Diamox:
The drug acetazolamide (trade name Diamox) can be used to reduce the effects of AMS, useful where big height gaina are unavoidable. It also helps periodic breathing. It works by speeding up acclimatisatiion, but does not mask the symptoms of AMS. You can still get AMS, HAPE and HAPE while taking it. Some people are allergic to Diamox. There are side effects, e.g tingling, especially of the heands, feet and face. Some people find this unpleasant, but it is not harmful and will go away when you stop taking the drug. It also make you pee even more.
